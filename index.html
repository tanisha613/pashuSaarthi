About Pashu Saarthi

The project “PASHU SARTHI: AN INTEGRATED LIVESTOCK IDENTIFICATION TRANSPORTATION AND HEALTH SERVICES PROJECT” focuses on the safe transportation of livestock and the owner can provide a proof of document for the security of their own self as well as the animals. Many regions lack a standardized and reliable system for identifying and tracking individual animals. Existing transportation methods for livestock are often unorganized and lack efficiency. Focused on addressing key challenges in livestock management, the project encompasses three crucial components: Livestock Identification, Transportation, and Health Services

Livestock Identification

This system shall also help minimizing the smuggling of animals by providing them unique id and linking this ID to their eye biometrics. A dedicated web page shall exist for each scanned animal ID, this web page shall open whenever bar code is scanned.

Livestock Transportation

Our system provides a solution on the ground level to regulate the movement of animals to weekly bazars, between cities, from one dairy to another, and movements under sale purchase of animals between different regions using a tagging and identification system.

Livestock Health

Scheduled health check-ups for livestock to monitor their overall well-being. Early detection of potential health issues to ensure timely intervention. Implementation of vaccination schedules to prevent the outbreak of diseases.
